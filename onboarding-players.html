Player Onboarding — Rosterly

Welcome to the team! As a player you set your availability for each game, see where you're batting and fielding, and follow your team through the season. This guide gets you set up.

📷 _Screenshot placeholder: Rosterly home screen for a player_

1. Getting in — register, then log in

Rosterly is invite-only, and everyone registers first. You'll get a player code from your captain or league admin.

First time — register:

  1. Open the Rosterly app and tap Create an account.
  2. Enter your phone number, your player code, and your name.
  3. Tap Create Account — you're registered as a Player.

Every time after — log in:

  • On the sign-in screen, enter your phone number + your player code.
📷 _Screenshot placeholder: Register screen, then Sign-in screen_
Note: You can't log in until you've registered. The code must match your role — a code for a different role won't log you in. If it says your number isn't registered, register first.

2. Join your league

Your captain adds you to their roster, and you join the league to see your games.

  1. Open the menu ☰.
  2. Find your league and tap Request Access.
  3. Your admin approves it — you'll get a notification when you're in.
📷 _Screenshot placeholder: league list with "Request Access" button_

3. Set your availability — My Games

The most important thing you do each week: tell your captain whether you can make each game.

  1. Open My Games (Home card or menu ☰).
  2. Tap an upcoming game.
  3. Set your status: In, Maybe, or Out.

Your captain sees these counts when building the lineup, so keep them current.

📷 _Screenshot placeholder: My Games with the In / Maybe / Out control_

4. See your lineup — My Lineup

Before a game, check where you're slotted.

  1. Open My Lineup (Home card or menu ☰).
  2. See your batting order spot and fielding position for the next game.
📷 _Screenshot placeholder: My Lineup showing batting + fielding_

5. Helping set the lineup — Huddle (co-captains only)

If your captain makes you a co-captain, you can join the Huddle — a shared room where you and the captain agree on the batting order and fielding before game day.

  1. Open Huddles from the menu ☰.
  2. Open the game's room — Chat, Batting, and Fielding tabs.
  3. Suggest changes to the draft (everyone's edits sync live) and drop 👍 / 👎 with notes.
  4. The captain finalizes it (or hands you the "gavel" to finalize).
📷 _Screenshot placeholder: Huddle room (Batting tab)_
Note: This only appears if you're a co-captain and your league has Huddles enabled. Regular players won't see it — that's normal.

6. Your stats & standings

After games finish, your stats update automatically, and you can follow the league.

  • Standings (Home card or menu ☰) — league standings, scores, and results.

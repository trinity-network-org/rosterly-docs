Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Rosterly ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Rosterly mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the app, we collect:

  • Phone number — used to identify your account.
  • Name (first and last) — displayed to teammates and administrators.
  • Role and league membership — your assigned role (player, captain, referee) and the leagues you participate in.
  • Game data — availability responses, lineup participation, and game results you submit.
  • Bug reports — if you submit a bug report, we collect your description and any optional photos you attach.
  • Device push token — to send you game reminders and league notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Usage data — anonymized event data (e.g., screens viewed, features used) to help us improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Rosterly service.
  • To send game reminders, lineup notifications, and league updates.
  • To allow administrators to manage rosters and league access.
  • To diagnose crashes and bugs and improve app stability.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure (United States).
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting.
  • Mixpanel — anonymized usage analytics.
  • Expo / AWS SNS — push notification delivery.
  • Flagsmith — feature flag management.

Your name and role are visible to other members of your league within the app. Your phone number is visible to league administrators only.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete Account); we remove it within 30 days. Your league game history is kept but anonymized so team and standings records stay intact. You may also request deletion by contacting us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Rosterly is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.
